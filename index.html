NativeFlow

Utility style-objects for React Native, to seamlessly switch from Tailwind on web to NativeFlow on app, no extra setup required, import and use!

Nativeflow Flash Setup ⏱️

Easy to Use

NativeFlow was built using JS literals to be easily installed and used to get your stylesheet up and running quickly

Focus on What Matters

NativeFlow lets you focus on your styling, and we'll do the syntax work. Go ahead and move your styles into the styles = {[ m.t_2, p.x_4]}.

Powered by TypeScript

Extend or customize your app layout by reusing these objects. Just a single npm installation and you're ready, no babel setup required
